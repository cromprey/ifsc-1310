Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

Progressive Enhancement is the idea to provide content and functionality to all users. When coding, we always start with HTML. This is because it provides a foundation for our code, but it also provides the users to have the content that they need!

Progressive Enhancement focuses on giving the experience through necessity. Most of the time, the user will click on your page because it provides the content that they are searching for, not just because it looks pretty. It is important to focus on the quality of your content because this is the most important step.
